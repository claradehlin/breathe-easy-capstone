emotional digestion

To regulate our emotions, we must first accept them by acknowledging their presence.

distress tolerance

Use the acronym TIPP to help with distress tolerance.

Temperature: Hold ice cubes or splash your face with cold water.

Intense Exercise: Do intense exercise to match your intense emotion. This will increase oxygen flow and decrease your stress levels.

Paced Breathing: Try box breathing. Breathe in for four seconds, hold for four seconds, breathe out for four seconds, hold for four seconds & repeat.

Paired Muscle Relaxation: Focus on tightening a group of muscles for five seconds as much as you can and then relaxing it. It will be more relaxed than before.

vulnerability factors

Managing vulnerability factors is a great way to make sure that you are giving your body what you need. This includes sleep, exercise, eating regularly, and taking your medication as prescribed.
